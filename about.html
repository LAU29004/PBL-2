About Us

Welcome to "Web of Flaws," a platform designed to empower you with the skills needed to identify, analyze, and mitigate common web vulnerabilities. We believe in hands-on learning and provide a controlled environment where you can safely test and enhance your understanding of cybersecurity concepts.

Our mission is to foster a community of ethical hackers, developers, and security enthusiasts who are passionate about making the digital world a safer place.
